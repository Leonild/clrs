Thanks, Fiona Gwendolyn Ginn Nielsen \cite{nielsen:03}; prove that
$P(n) \geq c\cdot2^n$:

\begin{align}
  P(n) &= \sum_{k=1}^{n-1}P(k)P(n-k)\\
  &\geq \sum_{k=1}^{n-1}c2^k\cdot c2^{n-k}\\
  &= c^2\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}2^k2^{n-k}\\
  &= c^2\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}2^n\\
  &= c^2(n-1)2^n\\
  &\geq c\cdot2^n
\end{align}
